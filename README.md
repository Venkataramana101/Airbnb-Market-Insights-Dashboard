# 🏡 Airbnb Market Insights Dashboard

## 📌 Project Title

**Airbnb Market Insights Dashboard | Power BI Business Intelligence Project**

---

# 📖 Short Description / Purpose

The **Airbnb Market Insights Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed in **Power BI** to analyze Airbnb listings, pricing, customer reviews, and host performance across multiple cities.

The primary objective of this project is to transform raw Airbnb data into meaningful business insights that help stakeholders understand market trends, identify growth opportunities, evaluate customer satisfaction, and support data-driven decision-making.

This dashboard enables users to explore listing distribution, pricing strategies, review performance, host quality, and market growth through interactive visualizations and key performance indicators (KPIs).

---

# 🛠️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was developed using the following tools and technologies:

* **📊 Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development and data visualization
* **🔄 Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing
* **📐 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Creation of calculated measures and KPIs
* **🗂️ Data Modeling** – Building relationships between tables for efficient analysis
* **📈 Interactive Visualizations** – Charts, Cards, Maps, Slicers, and KPIs
* **📊 Business Intelligence** – Converting raw data into actionable business insights

# ✨ Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem

The Airbnb marketplace generates a large volume of listing and customer review data. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult for business stakeholders to identify market trends, evaluate host performance, understand customer satisfaction, and make informed pricing decisions.

This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing a centralized view of Airbnb performance across multiple cities.

---

### • Goal of the Dashboard

To build an interactive dashboard that enables users to:

* Analyze Airbnb market growth over time
* Compare city-wise listing performance
* Evaluate room pricing trends
* Understand customer satisfaction through review analysis
* Compare Super Hosts with regular hosts
* Identify business opportunities using data-driven insights

---

# ❓ Key Business Questions

This dashboard answers several important business questions:

1. Which city has the highest number of Airbnb listings?
2. How has Airbnb listing growth changed over the years?
3. Which room type has the highest average price?
4. Which property types dominate the Airbnb marketplace?

6. How do Super Hosts compare with regular hosts?

# 💼 Business Impact & Insights

### 📍 Market Distribution

* Paris has the largest share of Airbnb listings, making it the strongest market in the dataset.
* Major metropolitan cities contribute significantly to overall platform activity.

---

### 📈 Market Growth

* Airbnb experienced rapid expansion during its early years.
* Listing growth peaked around **2015**, followed by slower growth due to increasing regulations and market maturity.
* External events such as the COVID-19 pandemic further impacted listing growth.

---

### 💰 Pricing Analysis

* Entire homes and apartments represent the largest share of listings.
* Hotel rooms command the highest average prices, indicating premium market positioning.

---

### 🌟 Customer Experience

* Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro demonstrate the highest customer satisfaction scores.
* Hong Kong and Istanbul have comparatively lower ratings, highlighting opportunities for service improvements.

---

### 👥 Host Performance

* Super Hosts account for a significant portion of successful listings.
* Their consistent performance contributes positively to guest satisfaction and overall platform quality.

---

### 🧹 Service Quality Insights

Analysis of review categories indicates:

* Communication consistently receives strong ratings.
* Check-in experience performs well across most cities.
* Cleanliness and Value for Money present the greatest opportunities for improvement.

---

# 📈 Business Recommendations

Based on the insights generated from this dashboard:

* Encourage more hosts to achieve Super Host status.
* Improve cleanliness standards to enhance guest satisfaction.
* Optimize pricing based on room type and regional demand.
* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing cities while supporting lower-performing markets.
* Use customer review feedback to improve service quality and increase repeat bookings.
* Monitor listing growth trends to identify emerging markets and investment opportunities.
